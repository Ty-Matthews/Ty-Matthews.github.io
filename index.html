Profile

Materials scientist, chemist, computer scientist, and data scientist working to combat climate change through materials advances in the areas of renewable energy generation and storage. I have many years of experience in electrochemical carbon dioxide reduction, hydrogen fuel cells, water electrolysis, redox flow batteries, ion exchange membranes, and thin film light absorbing semiconductors for photoelectrochemical applications. My computer science background includes n-tier client/server programming and ETL data engineering using Visual C/C++, SQL Server, Python, ASP, Javascript/HTML, among many others.
