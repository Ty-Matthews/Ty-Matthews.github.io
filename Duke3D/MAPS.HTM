Check these out:



park.zip 131k Great level made by Tyler Day. Excellent tile selection, great lighting job, challenging SE puzzles, and solid design. What more can I say... it's a blast!
aqua2.zip 69k Part two of Ben Roffelson's Aqua series. This is probably the best user map I've ever played. Awesome textures, fantastic lighting, tons of eye-candy, & beautifully designed. The only problem with it is there's just too much. If you play the hardest level on 486-class machines, it grinds to a halt. I haven't played it on a Pentium, but I'd bet it soars. If ya have a Pentium, get this level. Now.
screw-u2.zip 25k Great Dukematch-only level. Tons of ambient sounds, excellent textures, nice layout, and cool doors. It's just the right size for Dukematch.
ourhouse.zip 298k Level designed by Matthew Dean & Tony Polichroniadis, modelled after their college living quarters. It's strictly DukeMatch, as there are no monsters. Great new tiles, and very detailed sector editing. Check out the toaster and the microwave!
nihil01.zip 27k Level by NIHIL. Great dukematch level; excellent texture variety and well-laid out sectors.
dhouse.zip 21k Excellent level by Dave, modelled after his house, I guess, as there is no .txt file with this level. Great tile/sprite variety and realism. Dukematch only.
caves.zip 81k Huge level made by John "Kasai" Leonard. Lots of monsters, large sloping terrains, great variety, and challenging play. Single player only for now, but DukeMatch and Co-op are in the works. Includes hint file if you're having problems :)
outpost.zip 25k Awesome DukeMatch level made by Byron Collins. Great tile selection, excellent use of sectors over sectors, nice multiplayer traps, and excellent technical Build skills. It's DukeMatch only, and a total blast.
outpost2.zip 53k The second version of Byron's Outpost map, adding tons of neat features, including single-player support. A great sequel to his popular first edition.
